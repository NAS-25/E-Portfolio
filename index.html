Cybersecurity Overview

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to Security and Risk Management, exploring key concepts, frameworks, and practices essential for protecting information and assets. It examines threats, vulnerabilities, and risk assessment methodologies, emphasizing proactive strategies to mitigate risks in organisational contexts. Learners will gain insights into governance, compliance, and incident response while developing analytical and decision-making skills. Through readings, seminars, and practical exercises, students will understand the balance between security controls and business needs.
